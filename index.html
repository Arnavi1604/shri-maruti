Our SERVICES

Sheet metal work and laser cutting are essential processes in the manufacturing and fabrication industry. They involve cutting, shaping, bending, and assembling thin metal sheets into finished products or components used across various industries like automotive, aerospace, construction, and electronics. Sheet metal work allows for the creation of complex shapes and structures with high precision and strength. Laser cutting enhances this process by providing accurate and clean cuts using high-powered lasers, which ensures minimal material wastage. These processes are widely used to produce parts with consistent quality and tight tolerances. The combination of traditional sheet metal techniques and advanced laser cutting technology improves production efficiency and product durability

CUTTING SERVICES

Cutting is a key process in sheet metal work, involving various methods for precision and efficiency. Shearing cuts straight lines using a shearing machine, while plasma cutting uses a high-speed plasma torch for thick materials.

Read More

BENDING

Bending is used to shape metal sheets into desired angles and curves. Press brake bending forms precise bends using a press brake, while roll bending creates cylindrical or conical shapes by rolling the sheet.

Read More

PUNCHING

Punching is a process where a punch and die are used to create holes or shapes in metal sheets. It allows for high-speed, precise cutting of complex patterns and is widely used in mass production.

Read More

LASER WORKS

Laser cutting uses a high-powered laser beam to cut, engrave, or mark materials with high precision and minimal waste. It is ideal for creating intricate designs and clean edges in metal and non-metal sheets.
